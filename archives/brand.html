    • Brand

    The brand "Smart Black Panther Studios" is primarily about bringing the client's art to life! Whether it's digital or physical art, we can execute your idea. From 3D printed items to items made with clay, to paintings, to websites, the mediums are endless! Like animals, your art should be wild (and free)!

    This brand primarily uses the font Telegraf for headers and the font Garet for the body and its paragraphs. Primary Headers will use the color hot pink (#FF6ECF), secondary headers will use dark cyan (#046362), and the body and it's paragraphs will be purple (#AD56C3). There will also be use of more colors such as peach (#FE696E), blue (#0B6380), light blue (#74B0BF), green (#C7C804) and yellow (#F8C954) for added pops of color.
